# Keystone brand style guide

## Copy casing standard

All marketing and interface copy must use sentence case across the site.

- Use a capital letter only at the start of a sentence or label.
- Keep mid-sentence words lowercase unless they are proper nouns.
- Do not use title case for headings, buttons, nav items, tags, or section labels.
- Do not force all-caps through CSS `text-transform`.

## Examples

- Correct: `Book a consultation`
- Correct: `View our projects`
- Correct: `Selected work`
- Incorrect: `Book a Consultation`
- Incorrect: `VIEW OUR PROJECTS`
- Incorrect: `Selected Work`

## Scope

Apply this standard to homepage copy, shared components, and global styles so casing stays consistent on current and future pages.
